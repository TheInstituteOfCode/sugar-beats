ALL DAY DINING

Ocean Kitchen

Fresh from the sea, straight to your plate. Ocean Kitchen celebrates Bintan’s coastal bounty with dishes that spotlight the day’s catch and the flavors of the island. Whether it’s grilled seafood by the beach or inventive takes on Indonesian classics, every meal here is a nod to the fishermen and flavors that make Bintan unique.

Cocktails & Craft Infusions

Tiki Bar

Step into our iconic Tiki Bar and savor cocktails that embody the island's vibrant tropical flavours. Amidst bamboo and wooden architecture, enjoy neighbourhood inspired cocktails expertly crafted by mixologist Wirasada, hailing from Bali, that pay homage to the island's people and farmers.

A modern spin on traditional satay

SATAY BAR

STIX

Stix, a 16-seat restaurant & bar where we take the beloved Indonesian satay and infuse it with a modern twist. Using the freshest locally sourced ingredients and traditional techniques we honor the island’s rich culinary heritage while taking inspiration from global cuisine.
