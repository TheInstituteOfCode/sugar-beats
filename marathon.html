BINTAN MARATHON 9 - 11 NOV 2025

Join in the fun and get away for the weekend to a tropical island escape, running through pristine jungle & mangroves.

Experience the scenic beauty of Bintan on clean, flat roads, free from vehicles and filled with fresh air—perfect for a peaceful and energizing run. And then celebrated your race at the Runners Recharge Lounge at Sugarbeats.

Mandiri Bintan Marathon, happening on November 8–9, 2025, will welcome up to 5,000 runners to one of the world’s most beautiful races. From a 3km fun run to a full marathon, there is something for everyone as you run through ancient forests and around stunning lakes.

After the race, head to the recovery lounge at Sugarbeats on Lagoi Beach where you’ll find complimentary foot massage station & celebratory drinks (for all runners who completed a race). Listen to the beats of our live music, watch the sunset over the ocean and celebrate your race.

Runners Recharge Lounge

To celebrate the Bintan Marathon, we’re opening Sugarbeats up to the community with a runners recharge lounge - join us anytime from 4pm with complimentary foot massage station & celebratory drinks (for all runners who completed a race) and happy hour drinks for their supports & friends.

Stay all night and enjoy live music and celebrate your run at the unofficial after party of the Marathon.

-

Supporting the Clean Ocean Program

This year’s theme, “Run and Discover: Clean Oceans”, invites participants to take meaningful steps toward protecting our marine environment. To support this cause, a portion of each registration fee will go directly to ocean conservation efforts, including beach cleanups and marine education in Bintan.
