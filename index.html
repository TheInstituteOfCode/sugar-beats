AN OCEANFRONT BEACH CLUB IN BINTAN

Sugarbeats is Bintan’s best beach club, a spot to eat, drink and play and watch the world go past all with a dreamy ocean backdrop.

Set on one of Bintan’s most stunning stretches of beach, SugarBeats blends ocean views, curated music, and elevated dining into one unforgettable experience. By day, unwind by the water. By night, let the music move you.

{% include day-dreaming.html %}

SugarBeats is your barefoot escape on Bintan’s coast. Think lazy afternoons with a cold drink in hand, fresh bites straight from the kitchen, and live beats rolling in as the sun goes down. Take a dip in our oceanfront swimming pool, pick one of the countless daybeds to lounge on and watch the world go by.

In the evenings Sugarbeats comes alive, watch the sunset over the ocean and then listen to the beats, with live music every weekend and a curated playlist all week. Try one of our curated cocktails, and enjoy one of the best meals you’ll have in Bintan.

Ocean Kitchen

Fresh from the sea, straight to your plate. Ocean Kitchen celebrates Bintan’s coastal bounty with dishes that spotlight the day’s catch and the flavors of the island. Whether it’s grilled seafood by the beach or inventive takes on Indonesian classics, we've got something for everyone.

Stix
